Game of S.K.A.T.E

1. Play Rock Paper Scissors to decide who gos first.
2. Player 1 rolls a new trick; if they land the trick player 2 must land it to avoid getting a letter (S)
3. If player 1 misses the trick, it is player 2's turn to roll a new trick -
4. The first player to miss 5 of the other players tricks hence getting all letters S.K.A.T.E loses the game.

Gambling Game

1. Grab some friends and put some money up.
2. Ro sham bo for first.
3. Player 1 set a bet $$ and another player match it. it
4. Player 1 rolls a new trick, if player 1 lands the trick they get all the money betted. 5. If player 1 misses the trick, player 2 takes the pot.
6. REPEAT!
